Karma Yoga

The path of selfless action, where Krishna teaches to perform one's duty without attachment to the results.

Bhakti Yoga

The path of devotion, focusing on unconditional love and surrender to Lord Krishna.

Jnana Yoga

The path of knowledge, helping to understand the eternal truths and transcend ignorance.

Universal Compassion

Krishna's compassion for all beings transcends boundaries, teaching us to show love and kindness to everyone.

Detachment

The art of letting go of material desires and remaining unaffected by the fluctuations of life.

Dharma

The righteous duty to follow the path of justice and virtue in all aspects of life.

Equanimity

Maintaining balance in the mind, unaffected by success or failure, happiness or sorrow.

About Lord Krishna

Lord Krishna is the eighth incarnation of Lord Vishnu and a key figure in the Bhagavad Gita, known for his divine teachings and compassion.
